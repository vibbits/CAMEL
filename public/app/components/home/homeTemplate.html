CAMEL

CAMEL is an online platform that compiles details of studies employing evolution experiments. Through community-driven updates, it has the goal to capture the recent explosion of the field, to serve as a searchable and comparative reference to both novice researchers and veterans in the field and to allow for large-scale meta-analyses.

This Compendium of Adaptive Microbial Experiments in the Lab was initiated in following review:
Bram Van den Bergh, Toon Swings, Maarten Fauvart & Jan Michiels (2018) Experimental design, population dynamics and diversity in microbial experimental evolution . Microbiology and Molecular Biology Reviews (MMBR).

Please cite this review when you use the database.
